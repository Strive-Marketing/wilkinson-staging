Special Event Rentals

Restrooms That Impress

Your guests deserve more than a standard porta potty. Whether you’re hosting a wedding, a fundraiser, a private festival, or an upscale outdoor dinner, Wilkinson Portables has the right unit for the occasion. Our luxury restroom trailers offer flushing porcelain toilets, stainless steel sinks, air conditioning, and Bluetooth audio — everything you need to provide a premium restroom experience outdoors.

For events where presentation matters but a full trailer isn’t necessary, our Special Event Hand Wash Stations and ADA-compliant units keep your guests comfortable and your event running smoothly. Every unit arrives thoroughly cleaned, inspected, and fully stocked.

The Right Fit for Any Occasion

Special Event Restroom Rentals Across Northern California

Every event is different, and we take the time to match you with the right equipment. Smaller private gatherings might call for a single luxury trailer — a 14 x 9 four-station unit that accommodates 250–300 guests and comes equipped with air conditioning, a baby changing station, interior and exterior LED lighting, and Bluetooth audio. For mid-size events, our ADA porta potties and Special Event Hand Wash Stations offer an elevated experience without the footprint of a full trailer.

We handle everything from delivery and placement to servicing during multi-day events. Our team will work with your venue coordinator or event planner to make sure restrooms are positioned well and refreshed on schedule.

Whether you’re planning months out or need something in a hurry, give us a call or fill out the quote form and we’ll get you taken care of.
